About Me

My name is Jenny, and I'm delighted to share that my heart truly belongs to the holiday season, especially the unparalleled magic of Christmas time. It's an open invitation to cozy warmth: the gentle hush of snow days, the comforting ritual of a perfect cup of hot chocolate, the deep joy of gift-giving, and that incomparable festive atmosphere that wraps us all in cheer. While I happily watch my cherished Christmas movies year-round, there's nothing quite as wonderful as sinking into them when the actual season is here.
